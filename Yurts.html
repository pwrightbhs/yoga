The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a Yurt?
Our luxury yourts are permanent structures four feet off the ground. Each yurt has canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened.
How are the Yurts Furnished?
Eaech yurt is furnished with a queen-size bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove. The luxury camping experience also includes electricity and a sink how and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.
What should I bring?
Bring a sense of adventure and some time to relax! Most guests also pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with layers of clothing.
